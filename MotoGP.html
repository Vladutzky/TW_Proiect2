What is MotoGP

       The most competitive and high-level motorcycle racing in the world with the most audience and the best races.

When does it happen?

On a yearly basis by an established calendar which starts in march and ends in november, contains around 22 races in which the world championship is decided.

How to win it?

At the end of all races, based on a point system which gradually awards points, the one with the most points wins.
