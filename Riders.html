RIDERS

"I have never seen a bike that wins by itself."

What does it take?

As a rider you have to start around the age of 5-6 years old in order to get to the MotoGP, train a lot and have a good diet.

Where do they come from?

The two countries with the richest tradition in MotoGP are Italy and Spain, but we also have riders from the likes of Japan, Australia, South Africa or France

Isn't it dangerous?

It's in their instinct to fight until the end and to not give up, so it comes naturally.
