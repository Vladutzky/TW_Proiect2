CONSTRUCTORS

"Aero package is for the one who doesn't know how to make an engine"

What are exactly constructors?

They are the moto companies that provide bikes for every season. In 2022, they come from 3 countries : Japan(Honda,Yamaha,Suzuki), Italy(Aprilia,Ducati) and Austria(KTM).

What is the difference?

Each constructor has better sides, such as the engine speed of a Ducati, or the mobility of a Suzuki, and bad ones, so that every bike will behave different on track.

The matters of performance

Every season there is a standing for constructors and the winner takes the most money, which can invest in development, and so on.
